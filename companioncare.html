Companion Care Services

Kiku Home Health Care offers non-medical care services for seniors and individuals with disabilities in the comfort of their own homes.

Our companion care services provide social interaction, light housekeeping, meal preparation, errand running, medication reminders, and more.

We strive to provide compassionate and reliable care to improve our clients' quality of life.
